# Privacy Policy for AI Tutor

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2024  

AI Tutor respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect  

AI Tutor processes the following types of data to provide its services:  

### Token Data:  
- University information (e.g., "uvu").  
- Canvas ID.  

### Messages:  
- Text content for chat interactions.  
- User and assistant roles.  

### Course Data:  
- Course names, IDs, roles, and institution names.  
- Current scores (if available).  

### Assignment and Activity Data:  
- Assignment details (e.g., name, description, due date, points possible).  
- Activity stream, including submission status, scores, and comments.  

### User Profile Data:  
- First and last name, Canvas ID, institution, and profile avatar URL.  
- List of enrolled courses.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Non-Personal Use:** AI Tutor does not use your personal data for advertising, marketing, or unrelated purposes.
