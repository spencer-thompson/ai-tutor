# About AI Tutor
**Helping UVU Students Learn Smarter**

AI Tutor is a tool designed specifically for UVU students to make learning easier and more effective. By connecting directly with UVU Canvas, it helps students stay on top of their classes, get quick answers to questions, and focus on what really matters—learning.

## Why AI Tutor?  
We know how challenging it can be to manage schoolwork, especially with everything else going on in life. AI Tutor is here to make that easier. It gives students:  
- A simple way to access their Canvas assignments and grades.  
- Smart, AI-powered help to explain tricky topics.  
- Soon there will be a mobile app that works wherever you are, so you can learn on the go.

## Our Purpose  
Our goal is simple: help UVU students succeed by making learning more accessible, personal, and efficient. Whether you need help understanding a concept or just want a better way to manage your coursework, AI Tutor has you covered.
