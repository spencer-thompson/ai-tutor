# **Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date: November 18, 2024**

**At AI Tutor, we value the privacy and security of our users. This
Privacy Policy outlines the type of data we collect, how it is used, and
the measures we take to protect it. By using the AI Tutor system, you
agree to the terms outlined in this policy.**

---

## **1. Scope**

**This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the AI Tutor platform,
including students and faculty accessing the system through the Canvas
Learning Management System (LMS) or other integrated platforms.**

---

## **2. Data We Collect**

**AI Tutor processes the following types of data to provide its
services:**

### **2.1 User-Provided Data**

-   **Chat Interactions: Messages entered by users during AI Tutor
    sessions, including text content and roles (user and assistant).**

-   **User Identifiers: Unique, anonymized identifiers provided by the
    Canvas LMS to distinguish users.**

### **2.2 Course and Performance Data**

-   **Course Data: Names, IDs, roles, and institution names.**

-   **Assignment and Activity Data: Assignment details (name,
    description, due date, points possible), activity stream (submission
    status, scores, and comments), and current scores (if available).**

-   **User Profile Data: First and last name, Canvas ID, institution,
    profile avatar URL, and list of enrolled courses.**

### **2.3 System-Generated Data**

-   **Usage Analytics: Information about system interactions, such as
    session durations and feature usage, for performance analysis and
    improvement.**

-   **Technical Logs: Data on errors, system health, and API
    interactions.**

---

## **3. How We Use Your Data**

**Data collected by AI Tutor is used exclusively to enhance educational
outcomes and optimize the system's performance. Specifically:**

-   **Service Provision: To personalize learning experiences and deliver
    course-related insights.**

-   **System Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve system
    functionality.**

-   **Course Evaluation: To assist faculty in understanding aggregate
    student engagement and performance.**

**Non-Personal Use: AI Tutor does not use personal data for advertising,
marketing, or unrelated purposes.**

---

## **4. Data Sharing**

**We do not share any collected data with third parties for marketing or
external purposes. Data is only accessible to authorized project team
members under strict confidentiality.**

-   **Service Providers: Data may be processed by secure third-party
    tools necessary for the app's functionality.**

-   **Academic Oversight: Aggregate, anonymized data may be shared with
    faculty to enhance course planning and instruction.**

---

## **5. Data Storage and Retention**

-   **Storage: Data is securely stored on servers managed through
    [\[Hetzner Cloud\]].**

-   **Retention: Data is retained only for the duration necessary to
    fulfill the system's educational objectives. Chat logs are only
    stored manually by the user and deleted after [\[30 days\]].**

---

## **6. Data Security**

**We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data
from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.**

-   **Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS
    protocols.**

-   **Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to the data,
    protected by multi-factor authentication (MFA).**

-   **Anonymization: User data is de-identified wherever possible to
    minimize risks.**
    
---

## **7. Your Rights**

**As a user of AI Tutor, you have the following rights regarding your
data:**

-   **Access: You can request access to the data we have collected about
    you.**

-   **Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccuracies in
    your data.**

-   **Deletion: You can request deletion of your data, subject to
    applicable retention policies.**

-   **Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential data processing by
    notifying your course administrator.**

**For any of these actions, please contact ahmed.alsharif@uvu.edu.**

---

## **8. Children's Privacy**

**AI Tutor is designed for college-level students and does not knowingly
collect data from children under 13.**

---

## **9. Compliance with Data Privacy Laws**

**AI Tutor complies with relevant data privacy regulations, including:**

-   **FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act): Ensures the
    privacy of student education records.**

-   **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): For users within the
    European Economic Area.**
